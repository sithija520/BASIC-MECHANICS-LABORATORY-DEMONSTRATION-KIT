About Our Project & Team

The Mechanism Demonstrator Kit

This project aims to create a versatile and engaging Mechanism Demonstrator Kit. Designed for students from school to university levels, our kit provides a hands-on approach to understanding core mechanical engineering principles. Users can assemble, observe, and experiment with various fundamental mechanisms, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application.

The kit includes modules for demonstrating:

  • Gear and Pinion sets (Involute & Cycloidal)
  • Simple and Compound Gear Trains
  • Flat Belt and Pulley Systems (with tension measurement)
  • CAM Mechanisms (graphing follower motion)

The accompanying website serves as a comprehensive digital guide, offering assembly instructions, theoretical explanations, and experimental procedures to maximize the educational value.
